Coronavirus has slowed us down but don't stop

The role of technology in this Coronavirus

Technology has allowed science to make great strides in relatively short times, and along with it technological progress has moved, which together with: respirators, tampon machines and computers, has made it possible to mitigate the curve of contagions and help those sick to heal.

Who allowed all this are: doctors, nurses, engineers, technicians, computer scientists, systems engineers and many others, all have done their part and we can say that they have won a great battle, and that we have finally done it together.

Happiness in seeing the nearness

Another fundamental aspect that has not been well explained or that perhaps has not been given much weight is the ability that the Internet has had in bringing people closer and making them feel less alone, video calls but also the online publishing itself, newspapers , online courses, learning systems for distance schools, remote press conferences, teleworking or smartworking, all of these are just some of the advantages that internet and technology have brought to humanity and the environment, less cars on the road, it means: less pollution, less stress, less traffic and much more.

Everyone has lost something in this situation, but now it's time to give yourself strength, roll up your sleeves

and think about Future WITH Positivity!

Now don't miss this opportunity the market is waiting for its lash and that lash could be you!
